Setting Up Your Availability

Before you can start accepting appointments, you need to set up your counseling schedule. This is a quick 2-minute process that will:

  • 1

    Choose Your Working Days

    Select which days of the week you're available for counseling sessions. You can choose multiple days and modify this later.

  • 2

    Set Your Hours

    Specify your start and end times for each selected day. This helps students know when they can book appointments with you.

  • 3

    Individual Day Schedules (Optional)

    For more flexibility, you can set different hours for specific days. For example, shorter hours on Fridays or different times on Mondays.

Important Notes

  • You can change your availability anytime from your profile menu
  • Students can only book appointments during your available hours
  • You'll receive notifications for new appointment requests
  • Appointments are typically 45-60 minutes long
